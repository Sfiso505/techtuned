My Journey

My journey into the tech world has been anything but straightforward. After facing multiple setbacks, I discovered the transformative power of industry certifications and internships. I climbed the ladder from entry-level IT roles to a specialized focus in Data Science, which led to a pivotal internship at BCX (Business Connexion). This experience paved the way for my current role as a Data Scientist at Telkom, where I work beyond the traditional scope of data science on projects involving the latest technologies in Industry 4.0, such as hyper-connectivity that combines IoT, AI, and Digital Twins.

Now, I’m not just crunching numbers; I’m also guiding interns on exciting projects like Acoustic Modeling. Recently, I am furthering my studies in conjunction with work, having started to pursue my degree in Bachelor of Computer and Information Sciences.

When I’m not immersed in data, you can find me on the soccer field, producing music, playing table pool, and gaming.
